Our Origin Story

Fikanova is a name born from the intersection of creativity, technology, and future-forward thinking. Our brand philosophy is encoded in the three words that form our identity: Fikra, Arcane, and Nova.

Fikra

(Arabic: Idea/Imagination)

Represents the limitless potential of human imagination and the initial spark of innovation that drives every project.
